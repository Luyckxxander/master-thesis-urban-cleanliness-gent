- 3.4.3 Standardisation of analysis tables

## 3.5 Study area delineation and spatial coverage

## 3.6 Exploratory analysis of the datasets

- 3.6.2 Spatial coverage
- 3.6.3 Distribution of cleanliness scores

## 3.7 Analytical framework

- 3.7.2 Pattern agreement between CCI and NB
- 3.7.3 Agreement analysis (Bland–Altman)

- 3.7.5 Spatial analysis (Moran’s I and LISA)
